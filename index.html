WELCOME TO IDASS 2026

The 3rd lnternational Conference on Intelligent Data Analytics and Sustainable Systems (IDASS2026) will be held in Qingdao, China, on September 23-25th, 2026. Sept. 23th is the registration date, Sept. 24th to Sept. 25th are conference days.

The 2026 International Conference on Intelligent Data Analytics and Sustainable Systems aims to enhance the advanced technical development, bridge the gap between the two domains and foster discussions, collaborations and knowledge exchange among the experts with diverse backgrounds. We invite researchers, academicians, practitioners, policymakers and industry professionals to submit their original research papers, case studies and abstracts for consideration. This conference will create a multidisciplinary platform for participants to showcase and share their cutting-edge research findings, practical applications, experiences and insights related to intelligent data analytics and sustainable systems across a wide range of problem domains, theories, technologies and applications from software and hardware designs.

IMPORTANT DATES


Deadline for paper submission: May 11, 2026

Notification of paper acceptance: Jul. 11, 2026

Submission of camera-ready papers: Jul. 20, 2026

Deadline for early registration: Jul. 20, 2026

Deadline for registration: Aug. 20, 2026
